Project Overview

Luccman as Designer

Project: Canteen Menu Design

Location: Local School Canteen

In this project, I designed two large banners for a school canteen to display their menu. The designs were tailored to two specific audiences: kids and teachers. Each design reflects the unique preferences and needs of its target audience.

Designs

Kids' Menu

The kids' menu design features a light mode theme with playful background sketches of food items and school-related elements. The goal was to create a fun and engaging design that appeals to children.

Teachers' Menu

The teachers' menu design uses a dark mode theme with subtle random icons in the background. The design is sleek and professional, catering to the preferences of the teaching staff.

Design Process

Concept Development:

  • Conducted discussions with the canteen staff to understand their requirements and target audiences.
  • Created initial sketches and mood boards for both designs.

Design Creation:

  • For the kids' menu, used bright colors and hand-drawn sketches to create a playful and inviting look.
  • For the teachers' menu, used a dark color palette with clean typography and subtle iconography for a professional feel.

Implementation:

  • Coordinated with a local print shop to produce high-quality banners.
  • Ensured the banners were prominently displayed in the canteen for maximum visibility.

Key Features

  • Kids' Menu: Light mode with playful sketches and bright colors.
  • Teachers' Menu: Dark mode with clean typography and subtle icons.
  • High-Quality Printing: Ensured durability and vibrant colors for both designs.

Outcome

The designs were well-received by both the students and teachers, enhancing the canteen's atmosphere and making the menu more accessible and visually appealing.
