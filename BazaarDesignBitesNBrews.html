Design Project Overview

Luccman as Designer
Company: Lucra Studios
Project: Bazaar Design for Bites N Brews
Location: Port Dickson, Malaysia
In March 2025, during the 1st week of Ramadhan, I collaborated with a local businessman to create a vibrant visual identity for a new bazaar in Port Dickson. The project involved designing banners, food menus, and food displays to attract customers and enhance the shopping experience.

My Role

  • Banners: Designed eye-catching banners to promote the bazaar.
  • Food Menus: Created clear and attractive menus for food vendors.
  • Food Displays: Developed engaging layouts for food presentations.

Design Process

Concept Development:

  • Conducted initial meetings with the client to understand their vision and target audience.
  • Created mood boards to explore design ideas and color schemes.

Design Creation:

  • Developed simple and elegant designs using an off-white background to convey exclusivity.
  • Focused on clean typography and minimalistic layouts to enhance readability and visual appeal.

Implementation:

  • Coordinated with a local print shop to produce high-quality banners and menus.
  • Set up the banners on-site at the bazaar, ensuring they were prominently displayed to attract customers.

Key Features

  • Vibrant Colors: Used bright accents against an off-white background to create a festive yet exclusive atmosphere.
  • Clear Typography: Ensured readability for all signage and menus.
  • Cohesive Branding: Maintained a consistent visual theme across all materials.
